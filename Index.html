FACILITIES


The medical facility is fully equipped with doctor chambers, patient emergency rooms with ICU beds, and a dispensary for OTC medicines. In case of an emergency, an ambulance is also available on campus. Medications are available for headache, common cold, fever, body pains, sprains, treatment of minor injuries such as mild cuts, scrapes, or burns. Antivenom injections and seizure treatments are also available.
